Sources & References

Academic & Technical Sources

Key references, academic papers, and documentation used to compile this NLP repository.

  • Speech and Language Processing (3rd ed. draft) — Dan Jurafsky and James H. Martin. (Core reference for Text Preprocessing, RNNs, LSTMs, and N-grams).
  • Attention Is All You Need — Vaswani et al., 2017. (Foundational paper for Transformers, Attention mechanisms, and BERT).
  • Efficient Estimation of Word Representations in Vector Space — Mikolov et al., 2013. (Foundational paper for Word2Vec word embeddings).
  • Enriching Word Vectors with Subword Information — Bojanowski et al., 2017. (Foundational paper for FastText representations).
  • Hugging Face, LangChain, and PyTorch Documentation — Industry standard APIs for RAG, contextual embeddings, and pipelines.
