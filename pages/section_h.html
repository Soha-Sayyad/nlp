Reflection Note A — Soha Sayyad

Roll No. 61

Through studying concepts like BERT, One-Hot Encoding, and the interactive Preprocessing Pipeline, I broadened my understanding of how unstructured text is normalized, indexed, and converted into dense vector representations. Focusing on the Feature Engineering Pipeline and the Language Model Architecture Pipeline gave me deep insights into sequential models like LSTMs compared to classical statistical approaches.
